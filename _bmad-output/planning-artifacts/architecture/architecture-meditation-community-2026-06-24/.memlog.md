- (constraint) Greenfield Flutter-first mobile scope; iOS-first differentiators must not break core value for users without wearable or health permissions.
- (decision) Use a modular hexagonal architecture with an offline-first Flutter mobile app and a thin managed backend; domain slices own their aggregates and adapters isolate platform and cloud services.
- (decision) Make session timeline events the single source of truth for continuity, reflection inputs, and analytics instead of mutating many summary records directly.
- (decision) Normalize all noise and health signals behind permission-gated sensing adapters; never upload raw microphone audio and do not require wearable data for any core flow.
- (decision) Use ephemeral aggregate presence only: no follow graph, no public profiles, no free-form chat, and no retention of precise co-meditation relationships as product data.
